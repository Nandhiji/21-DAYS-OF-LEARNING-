Step 1: What is HashSet?

👉 HashSet = Collection that stores UNIQUE values

No duplicates allowed ❌
Example
Input: 10, 20, 10, 30

HashSet stores:

[10, 20, 30]
Duplicate 10 is removed automatically.

HOW TO CREATE HASHSET:

HashSet<Integer> set =new HashSet<>();
